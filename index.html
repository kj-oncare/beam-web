Head of Business Development


beam is looking for a Head of Business Development for Germany to join our team. Successful candidates will bring a dynamic combination of IQ and EQ, an ability to think strategically and execute tactically, and strong interpersonal skills to maximize value. Someone who has the oversight and isn’t afraid to manage multiple deals in a high-pressure, high-stakes environment. Essentially, a business athlete with ambition and drive, capable of building partnerships to support growth and value creation.

Reporting directly to the founders, you will get responsibility from day one, can prove yourself in a highly dynamic environment and actively shape the development of our business with full power. When the day comes, we expect you to assume the role of CEO without hesitation.



What you'll do:

  • Build strategic relationships with key stakeholders in the municipal, mobility, tech, and finance ecosystems, including cities, investors, large corporates, and other startups, to stay on top of industry trends and the mobility competitive landscape

  • Independently structure and implement complex projects with numerous stakeholders

  • Conceptualize and execute growth initiatives and develop market strategies for Germany and European countries

  • Plan and implement processes for scalable topline development

  • Drive complex, cross-functional strategic initiatives that impact all aspects of the business and organization

  • Manage fundraising processes, including the production of marketing materials, meeting with prospective investors and working on execution


What You'll need:

  • 2 to 4 years of experience at in a fast-paced professional environment (i.e. High-Growth Start-Up, Tech-Giant, Strategic Consulting or Corporate Development) with exposure to IP, software and technology

  • A hands-on entrepreneurial “can do” attitude and passion about moving things forward, taking full ownership of projects and driving them with full force

  • Strong interpersonal skills an experience in cultivating, negotiating, and growing business partnerships

  • A a high degree of comfort with analytics, numbers and the capability to draw decisions based on both intuition and data, strong financial skills

  • Excellent judgment, positive attitude, hustle, demonstrated a history of success

  • Adept in building and delivering presentations

  • Strong Excel and PowerPoint skills

  • Outstanding academic credentials

  • Fluency in German and English
